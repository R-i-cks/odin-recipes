Peanut Butter Fudge

Description

A gorgeous dessert or afternoon-ish snack to share with your friends.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 16 ounce package og brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of milk (dairy or vegetable)
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 and 1/2 cups of confectioners' sugar

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat
  2. Stir in brown sugar and milk
  3. Bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes, stirring frequently
  4. Remove from heat
  5. Stir in peanut butter and vanilla
  6. Pour over confectioners' sugar in a large mixing bowl
  7. Beat until smooth
  8. Pour into an 8x8 inch dish
  9. Chill until firm and cut into squares
  10. Enjoy :)
