Marguerita

Description

The best way to start your weekends

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces tequila
  • 1 fluid ounce orange flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau®)
  • ½ fluid ounce lime juice
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 lime wheel

Steps:

  1. Sprinkle salt on a small plate. Lightly wet the rim of a cocktail glass or margarita glass with a damp paper towel
  2. Dip the moistened rim in salt to coat. Set aside
  3. Combine tequila, orange-flavored brandy, and lime juice in a cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake until chilled
  4. Strain into a salt-rimmed cocktail glass or a salt-rimmed, ice-filled margarita glass
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel
  6. Enjoy :)
